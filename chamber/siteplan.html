Chamber of Commerce Site Plan


Purpose

The business members benefit the most from a chamber of commerce, but the community as a whole can also benefit. By supporting local businesses and helping them grow, the organization gives community members access to more shopping and services. It can help the area thrive and make it easier for consumers to find what they need.

Audience

Our members range from the small businesses and chambers of commerce across the country that support their communities, to the leading industry associations and global corporations that innovate and solve for the world's challenges, to the emerging and fast-growing industries that are shaping the future.

Scenarios

How can I contact the chamber's board of directors?

What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?

What resources does the chamber offer to help small businesses grow and succeed?

What has been the population growth in the area over the past few years?

What are the benefits of becoming a member of the chamber?

How can local businesses get involved in community events and initiatives organized by the chamber?
